1. The factO fatoEl hecho

On June 1, 2026, GitHub Copilot stops charging per request. Premium Request Units retire. GitHub AI Credits take over. 1 AI Credit equals US$ 0.01. Input, output and cached tokens are accounted for at the published API rate of each model.

2. The thesisA teseLa tesis

Token-based billing rewards disciplined engineering. Teams that treat tokens as a finite resource, apply context engineering, architect agents with the right pattern, and instrument observability will pay between 30% and 70% less than teams that use Copilot as a black box.
A cobrança por tokens recompensa engenharia disciplinada. Times que tratam tokens como recurso finito, aplicam context engineering, arquitetam agentes com o padrão certo, e instrumentam observabilidade vão pagar entre 30% e 70% menos que times que usam Copilot como caixa-preta.
La facturación por tokens recompensa la ingeniería disciplinada.
