1. The factO fatoEl hecho

AI agents do not change one role. They change 24 roles, with different intensity at different times. The product manager, the staff engineer, the SRE, the data engineer, the support specialist, all have a different agentic horizon. Treating "AI for engineering" as a single roadmap is a category error.
