About this playbook

Who wrote this, why, and what to do with it.

3 min read 5 sections

Author

Paula Silva, AI-Native Software Engineer. Pioneering software development with Agentic DevOps Platform.

Eight years in enterprise software, the last three building agent platforms for Fortune 500 customers across Latin America. Based in São Paulo, working across the Americas.

Why this playbook exists

Most enterprise teams in 2026 are sitting at Stage 2 of agent maturity, agents in production, no platform team, no shared vocabulary, no integration story. This playbook is the smallest set of patterns that gets a team from Stage 2 to Stage 4 without rebuilding the stack twice. It is opinionated, sourced, and meant to be argued with.

How to use it

Read the introduction, then go straight to the chapter for the layer you are weakest at. Use the maturity self-assessment at the end of Chapter 05 to confirm. Most readers need two, maybe three chapters. Do not attempt all five linearly unless you are setting up the stack from scratch.

Every chapter ends with a Monday morning list. Five things you can do this week with no new budget. Start there.
