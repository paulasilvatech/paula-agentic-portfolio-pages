24 personas for the agent-augmented software lifecycle.

A field-tested role catalog for teams delivering with AI assistants, Copilot and autonomous agents. Each persona comes with a playbook, an MCP manifest, and a ready-to-run kit.

Explore personas → Read the framework

Why roles need to evolve

Agents change who does what. A developer is no longer just writing code, a QA is no longer just writing tests, a product owner is no longer just writing stories. These 24 personas capture the new distribution of responsibility when AI is in the loop.

The nine clusters

Each cluster groups personas that share an accent color, a phase of the SDLC, and a failure mode.
