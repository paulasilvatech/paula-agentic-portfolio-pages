Reversibility plus business impact yield four quadrants for human review: full autonomy, sample audit, every-action review, human owns. Most enterprise agents belong in quadrant 2 or 3, not quadrant 1. Quadrant 1 is the smallest band and demands the most rigorous evals.Reversibilidade e impacto de negócio formam quatro quadrantes para revisão humana: autonomia total, auditoria por amostra, revisão por ação, humano decide.
